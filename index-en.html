About Potanity

Potanity is the official game development club at Kangwon National University. We bring together students interested in game planning, programming, graphics, sound, and more to create creative and innovative games. Our activities include Unity/Unreal projects, regular seminars, game jams, and mentoring. Anyone passionate about game development is welcome!

Club History

  • 2017: Potanity club founded
  • 2018~2022: Various in-house game projects and participation in competitions
  • 2023: Launched large-scale Unity project, recruited many new members
  • 2024: Official homepage and representative game released
  • 2025: WebGL-based game service and national university game jam planned
