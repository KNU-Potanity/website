About Potanity

Potanity is the official game development club at Kangwon National University.
Students interested in game planning, programming, graphics, sound, and more come together
to create creative and innovative games.

We work on Unity projects,
hold regular study sessions, meetings, planning studies, participate in exhibitions and game jams, and more.
Anyone passionate about game development is welcome!

Potanity Mini Games

Check out the mini games developed by Potanity!

Mini Game 1: Metal Slug

An action shooting game inspired by the classic Metal Slug. Clear various stages and defeat enemies with different weapons!

Mini Game 2: Reaction Speed Test

Click as quickly as possible when the signal appears to test your reaction speed! Challenge your reflexes.

Mini Game 3: Watermelon Game (In Development)

A puzzle game where you stack fruits to make a watermelon! Stack strategically and aim for the highest score.

Mini Game 4: Pico Park (In Development)

A multiplayer puzzle game where cooperation is key! Work together with friends to clear the stages.

Mini Game 5: Motion Recognition (In Development)

A motion recognition game using your webcam! Move your body to complete various missions.
